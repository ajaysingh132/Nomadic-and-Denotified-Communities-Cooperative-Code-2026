Vision

To establish a democratic, community-owned, family-centered and digitally enabled cooperative framework that promotes dignity, equality, self-reliance and sustainable development for Nomadic, Semi-Nomadic and Denotified Communities.

---

Mission

- Strengthen families through cooperative institutions.
- Promote community-led governance.
- Develop cooperative education, healthcare and entrepreneurship.
- Build transparent digital cooperative systems.
- Encourage research, innovation and social justice.
- Preserve cultural heritage while embracing modern technology.
